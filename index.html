WHO WE ARE

Students who believe change begins with one conversation.

Teal Trails is a student-run initiative dedicated to improving access, awareness, and understanding of women's healthcare in India — with a strong focus on cervical cancer prevention and breaking stigma.

We believe healthcare should never be a challenge to pursue. It should reach every woman, regardless of where she lives or what she earns.

WHAT WE DO

Our Initiatives

School Awareness Campaigns

Interactive sessions educating young girls and boys about cervical cancer, HPV, and the power of early vaccination.

HPV Vaccination Drives

On-ground vaccination camps delivering free and subsidized HPV vaccines to communities that need them most.

Mobile Health Camps

Bringing screening, awareness, and early detection directly to underserved areas across India.

Breaking the Stigma

Creating safe spaces for honest conversations about women's health through community dialogue.

The Complete Awareness Guide

How Cervical Cancer Develops

Nearly 99.7% of cervical cancers are caused by persistent high-risk HPV infection. This is a slow, detectable, and interruptible process.

STAGE 1
HPV Infection

Most infections clear naturally. Persistent infection with high-risk types begins cellular damage over time.

STAGE 2
Pre-cancer (CIN)

Detectable through screening. Fully treatable with simple outpatient procedures like cryotherapy or LEEP.
